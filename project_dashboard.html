MLB Interactive Dashboard

National League Central Analysis (Tableau)

Project Walkthrough

Overview: This dashboard focuses on the National League Central teams (Reds, Cardinals, Brewers, Cubs, Pirates). It visualizes player batting averages, On-Base Percentage (OBP), and Slugging Percentage (SLG) for the month of June.

Key Features:

  • Interactive Filtering: Users can filter by specific teams (e.g., clicking on "Reds") to see only that team's roster.
  • Drill-Down Capability: Clicking on an individual player (e.g., Matt McLain) updates the charts to show his specific contribution, such as his 5 Home Runs and .333 OBP for the month.
  • Trend Analysis: The dashboard tracks month-over-month changes, showing how metrics like OBP and SLG have improved or declined compared to May.

"Right now this dashboard is showing all the NL Central teams... You can use this slider to change the month... If you want to look at stats at an individual level, you can click on any player, like Matt McLain, and it updates the graphs to show his specific performance."
